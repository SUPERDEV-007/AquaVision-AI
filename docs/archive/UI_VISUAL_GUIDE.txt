SCENARIO 1: "I want to see WHERE the AI detects disease"
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2. Toggle ON: 🔥 Grad-CAM Heatmap
3. Click 🔍 Analyze
4. RESULT: Image shows with colored heatmap overlay!
   - Red areas = AI focusing here (disease symptoms!)
   - Blue areas = AI ignoring

SCENARIO 2: "I want to check my detection history"
───────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click 📊 History button
2. RESULT: Popup shows all past detections
3. Scroll through your complete history
4. See total count at bottom

SCENARIO 3: "I want desktop notifications for diseases"
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click 🔔 Alerts button
2. Click "Test Desktop Notification"
3. RESULT: Popup appears on your desktop!
4. Automatic alerts for critical diseases

SCENARIO 4: "I want to analyze multiple fish at once"
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Toggle ON: 🐟 Multi-Fish Mode
2. Upload image with multiple fish
3. Click 🔍 Analyze
4. RESULT: Each fish detected and analyzed separately!
